Common Questions

How do I change my daily goal?
Open Gulp, tap the Settings tab, and adjust your daily goal using the stepper.

Why aren't my reminders working?
Make sure reminders are enabled in the Settings tab, and that notifications are allowed for Gulp in your device's Settings app under Notifications.

How do I sync across devices?
Gulp syncs automatically through your iCloud account. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all your devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled.

Does Gulp collect my data?
No. Gulp stores everything on your device, with optional iCloud sync through your own Apple ID. We never see your data.
